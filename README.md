# E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional performance using Business Analysis techniques, SQL, Python, and Power BI.

The objective is to identify:

* High-performing products
* Valuable customer segments
* Sales trends
* Regional opportunities
* Profitability improvement areas

## Business Problem

The company is experiencing:

* Increasing sales but declining profits
* Low customer retention
* Uneven regional performance

Management requires data-driven recommendations to improve business performance.
