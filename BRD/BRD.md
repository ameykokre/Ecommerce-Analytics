# Business Requirements Document

## Project Title
E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics

## Problem Statement

The company is experiencing:

- Declining profits despite increasing sales
- Low customer retention
- Uneven regional performance

Management requires data-driven insights to improve profitability and customer retention.

## Business Objectives

1. Increase profit margin
2. Improve customer retention
3. Identify high-value customers
4. Optimize marketing investment
5. Improve regional sales performance

## Success Metrics

- Revenue Growth
- Profit Margin
- Customer Retention Rate
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Repeat Purchase Rate
